Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Welcome to YinsMgmt. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: If you sign in using Google, Apple, or LINE, we receive basic profile information (such as your name and email address) to manage your account and sync your data.
  • Health & Nutrition Data: Sugar intake records, food photos (if uploaded), and health metrics (weight, blood glucose, etc.) that you manually enter or sync via Health Connect.
  • Device Information: Basic device details and OS version to ensure app compatibility and for error tracking (via Sentry).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide core features like sugar intake tracking and AI nutritional analysis.
  • To sync your personal records across devices.
  • To calculate personal reward points and manage your "Pet" status within the app.
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs through anonymous error reporting.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide advanced features, we integrate with the following trusted providers:

  • Supabase: For secure database storage and user authentication.
  • Google Generative AI (Gemini): To provide AI-based nutritional estimations.
  • AdMob: To display non-intrusive advertisements for free users.
  • Sentry: For real-time error monitoring and crash reporting.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete specific intake records or health data at any time within the app.
  • You may request full account deletion through the "Settings" menu, which will permanently remove all your personal information from our servers.

5. Security

We implement professional security measures to protect your data. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using Industry-standard SSL technology.
